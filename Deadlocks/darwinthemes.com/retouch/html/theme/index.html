About DeadLocks

It is common in computer networks today to have abnormal traffic behaviors such as attacks, failures and malfunctions. Such abnormal behaviors impact on performance and network security, and cost financial losses. The detection of behavior in the network traffic, which triggers a thorough check of network attacks. The traffic flow is one of the most important information sources for behavior monitoring and abnormal behavior detection. The results show that it can detect network traffic anomalies with the group behavior characteristics. There are many network anomalies, but occurs whenever a network anomaly, behavior will change a shortly. These changes can be inspected by tracking various parameters of traffic flows. Not only monitor parameters such as flow fundamental flow size, number of packets, representing the main flow characteristics, also statistically collect certain extended parameters in order to understand flow trends connection. Due to rapid growth of the use of Internet and its related technologies, the demand for high quality and reliable internet service has increased. By looking at the above challenges it is imperative to carry out a thorough investigation to understand the abnormal behavior in network traffic for better detection.
